The dataset contains information collected during telemarketing activities carried out by a banking organization. Each record describes a customer and the outcome of a marketing campaign aimed at promoting term deposit products. The objective is to build predictive models capable of identifying customers who are more likely to subscribe to the offered financial product.
The classification goal is to predict if the client will subscribe a term deposit (variable y).

   Input variables:
   # bank client data:
   1 - age
   2 - job: type of job 
   3 - marital: marital status - note: "divorced" means divorced or widowed
   4 - education: education level
   5 - default: has credit in default?
   6 - balance: average yearly balance, in euros 
   7 - housing: has housing loan?
   8 - loan: has personal loan?
   9 - contact: contact communication type 
  10 - day: last contact day of the month
  11 - month: last contact month of year
  12 - duration: last contact duration, in seconds
  13 - campaign: number of contacts performed during this campaign and for this client
  14 - pdays: number of days that passed by after the client was last contacted from a previous campaign, -1 means client was not previously contacted
  15 - previous: number of contacts performed before this campaign and for this client
  16 - poutcome: outcome of the previous marketing campaign
  17 - y - has the client subscribed a term deposit?
